About Gamepedia

Gamepedia is a new and upcoming forum and news outlet for games.

Users can share everything ranging from gaming experiences opinions to news and innovations regarding both old and fresh games.

The friendly and outgoing community is here for everyone facing difficulties overcoming a level in their singleplayer games or join them in accomplishing online success in all game genres.
